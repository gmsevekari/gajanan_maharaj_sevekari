### About the Gajanan Maharaj Sevekari App

**Our Mission**

The Gajanan Maharaj Sevekari app is a humble offering dedicated to the devotees of Shri Gajanan Maharaj. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and easily accessible digital tool for your daily worship, spiritual reading, and devotional needs. We aim to connect devotees with the sacred teachings and traditions of Maharaj, no matter where they are.

**What This App Offers**

*   **Daily Devotion:** Access a complete collection of essential texts, including the *Gajanan Vijay Granth*, various *Stotras*, *Aartis*, and the *Namavali*.
*   **Soulful Bhajans:** Immerse yourself in a library of devotional *Bhajans* to uplift your spirit.
*   **Event Calendar:** Stay informed about important dates, festivals, and events celebrated in the Seva Marg.
*   **And More:** Discover features designed to support your devotional journey.
